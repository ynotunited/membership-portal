# GAFCONL Platform

A full-stack cooperative management platform built for Global Apex Farmers Cooperative Nigeria Limited (GAFCONL).

The platform provides a complete digital ecosystem for managing cooperative members, registrations, payments, community engagement, reporting, and administrative operations.

🌐 Live Application:
https://globalapexfarmers.org.ng/app/public/login


## 🚀 Overview

GAFCONL Platform is a custom-built membership management solution designed to digitize cooperative operations.

It enables organizations to manage their members, financial activities, communication channels, and internal workflows from a centralized platform.


## ✨ Key Features

### Member Management
- Online member registration
- Email verification workflows
- Member profiles

- Account management

### Financial Management
- Membership dues tracking
